About the Artist

Nkchruma Rashad Dardy

Creator behind NRD ART, blending graffiti aesthetics with bold visual storytelling. Based in South Florida, I'm here to create and inspire through art that speaks to identity, culture, and the raw energy of urban expression.

From intimate Paint & Sip experiences to large-scale exhibitions like "America is a Trap House," every piece and event is designed to ignite creativity and build community through artistic expression.
